🎓 N-SET Details

Important information for students & parents

🌟 What is N-SET? (Full Form)

N-SET stands for Nobel Scholarship Entrance Test. It is a scholarship-based entrance examination conducted by Shahada’s Nobel Academy for students aspiring to prepare for NEET, IIT-JEE & MHT-CET. The exam is designed to identify talented and deserving students and support them with scholarship benefits.

🌟 N-SET Scholarship Exam 2026 – Key Highlights

Through the N-SET Scholarship Exam 2026, students can avail scholarships ranging from ₹4,000 to ₹40,000, which are directly adjusted in the course fees. Higher performance in the exam results in a higher scholarship amount. This initiative follows the principle: “Score High & Pay Less.”

🌟 Courses Covered Under N-SET

  • 📘 Full Course – Class 11th & 12th (NEET / JEE / MHT-CET)
  • 📗 Separate Course – Class 11th (NEET / JEE / MHT-CET)
  • 📙 Separate Course – Class 12th (NEET / JEE / MHT-CET)
  • 🔁 NEET Repeater Course

🌟 Minimum Marks Required for Scholarship

To avail any scholarship under N-SET, a student must score a minimum of 30 marks in the examination. Students scoring below 30 marks are not eligible for scholarship benefits. Higher marks lead to higher fee concessions.

🌟 Parent Counselling & Career Guidance

Parent counselling is an essential part of the N-SET process. Our academic counsellors guide parents and students regarding course selection, fee structure, scholarship adjustment, and long-term career planning for medical and engineering streams. This ensures clarity, transparency, and confident decision-making.

🌟 Why N-SET Matters

N-SET is not just an exam, but a gateway to structured preparation, expert mentorship, and affordable quality education. It motivates students to perform better and helps parents plan their child’s academic journey with confidence.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Common questions related to N-SET

Q1. Who can apply for N-SET?
✅ Students studying in Class 7th to 12th and NEET repeater students are eligible to apply.

Q2. Is N-SET compulsory for admission?
✅ N-SET is mandatory for availing scholarship. Admission without scholarship is possible as per academy policy.

Q3. What is the minimum marks required for scholarship?
✅ Students must score a minimum of 30 marks in N-SET to become eligible for scholarship benefits.

Q4. How is the scholarship amount decided?
✅ Scholarship amount depends on the marks obtained in N-SET. Higher marks lead to higher scholarship amounts.

Q5. Is parent counselling compulsory?
✅ Yes. Parent counselling is compulsory to ensure transparency, correct course selection and proper academic planning.

Q6. How will I know my scholarship amount?
✅ Scholarship details will be informed during counselling and admission confirmation.
